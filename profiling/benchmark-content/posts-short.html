A short post is a good unit of work for the latest-posts loop on the homepage — five hundred bytes of body, nothing fancy, a single block-level paragraph and maybe a list. It exercises the render path once, keeps parse_blocks cheap, and acts as the baseline single-post scenario.

  • Fast to render
  • Minimal block tree
  • Matches most real blog posts

Special chars for kses: & < > "quoted" &amp; 🚀 — plus an internal link and an external one so the URL sanitizer has something to chew on.
